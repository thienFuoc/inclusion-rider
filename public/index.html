The Inclusion

RIDER

For Best Hiring Practices in Cultural Industries

The Inclusion Rider is a template and a contract provision that implements widely applicable best practices for building workplace diversity.

THE FOUR KEY

 COMPONENTS

THAT THE INCLUSION RIDER CONTAINS

A commitment to deepening and diversifying hiring pools

Establishing benchmarks or targets for hiring

Collecting, measuring and analyzing application and hiring

Implementing accountability measures to support progress if you fall short of your goals
